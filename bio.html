Biography

I enrolled at Tufts University in the fall of 2016 as an undergraduate engineering student. Originally a chemical engineering major, I changed my major to computer science after taking the intro course in the spring of my freshman year. I interned at ForgeAI the following summer, a startup located in Central Square which focuses on developing artificial intelligence models for natural language processing. I returned to Forge the following winter break. I am now in the second semester of my sophomore year at Tufts.
